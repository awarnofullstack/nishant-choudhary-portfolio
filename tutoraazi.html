Tutorazzi

A smart tuition app for kids who
is in international curriculums

Tutorazzi is a platform where students can connect with tutors worldwide
across various international curriculums. The app supports students,
parents, and teachers with a tailored, user-friendly interface that simplifies
booking sessions, tracking progress, and finding the right tutors.

Research Process üßê

Working closely with business owners and receiving insights from our project manager, I conducted a competitor analysis to understand the current solutions and identify gaps. Through initial feedback sessions, we gained critical insights into what each user needed, their pain points, and their primary frustrations. Insights

  • Students needed a quick, curriculum-based tutor search and a smooth booking flow.
  • Parents required easy access to track their child's progress and manage sessions.
  • Teachers needed a flexible profile setup with availability management and a rating system.
