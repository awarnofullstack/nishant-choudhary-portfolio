Research and Discovery

Research Process üßê

Working closely with business owners and receiving insights from our project manager, I conducted a competitor analysis to understand the current solutions and identify gaps. Through initial feedback sessions, we gained critical insights into what each user needed, their pain points, and their primary frustrations.
Insights

  • Students needed a quick, curriculum-based tutor search and a smooth booking flow.
  • Parents required easy access to track their child's progress and manage sessions.
  • Teachers needed a flexible profile setup with availability management and a rating system.
Competitor Analysis üßê

We examined platforms like VIPKid and Varsity Tutors, focusing on how they enable flexible learning schedules and curriculum filtering, which informed our design strategy for Tutorazzi.

  • Often struggled to find tutors with the exact expertise in their curriculum.
  • Needed simple tools to track performance and communicate with teachers.
  • Sought an intuitive profile setup and flexible scheduling options.
