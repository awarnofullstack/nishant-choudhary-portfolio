Helpbharat is a donation platform that enables users to contribute to various causes, including medical emergencies, education support, and disaster relief. The app allows people to make impactful donations and connect with individuals in need across India.

How it Works

  • Create an Account

    Users sign up and create their profiles to start exploring available fundraisers and causes.

  • Browse and Select Fundraisers

    Users can browse through multiple categories, such as medical emergencies, education, and disaster relief, and select a fundraiser they wish to support.

  • Make a Donation

    Once a fundraiser is chosen, users can donate directly through secure payment options available within the app.

  • Track Donation Impact

    Users can see updates from the fundraisers they have supported, providing transparency and a sense of connection to the cause.

Research and Discovery

Research Process üßê

Our research focused on understanding the motivations and pain points of individuals who are inclined to donate. By analyzing competitor platforms and interviewing potential users, we identified key features that enhance trust, transparency, and ease of use in the donation process.

  • Trust: Users are more likely to donate if the app is transparent about fund distribution.
  • Ease of Use: A smooth, quick donation flow was crucial for user retention.
  • Customization: Many users wanted the flexibility to choose the type and amount of their donation.
Competitor Analysis üßê

We analyzed popular platforms like Ketto and GoFundMe to identify areas for improvement, particularly around trust-building, seamless navigation, and personalized donation options.

  • Trust Issues: Users worry about the transparency of where their donations go.
  • Complexity in Finding Causes: Users found it hard to search for specific causes on existing platforms.

Reflection and Key Learnings

Challenges üßê

One of the main challenges was creating a sense of trust and transparency for users. Balancing an emotional appeal with a straightforward interface required careful design choices.

Learnings üßê

The research highlighted the importance of transparency in donation platforms. Providing impact updates helps foster trust and repeat engagement from donors.
