Homeserve is a comprehensive app connecting users with trusted professionals for various home services, including plumbing, electrical work, painting, and home spa services. Designed for busy individuals who want reliable, efficient, and safe home services at their fingertips.

How it Works

  • Create an Account

    Users can sign up and create their profiles to start browsing available services.

  • Browse and Book Services

    Users select the service they need and view profiles of service providers in their area, with real-time availability and transparent pricing.

  • Track and Manage Appointments

    The app provides live tracking of service providers on the way, and users can manage bookings and reschedule if needed.

  • Post-Service Feedback

    After the service is completed, users can rate the provider and leave feedback to improve future experiences.
